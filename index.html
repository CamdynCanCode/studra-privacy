Studra Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Studra ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: username, email address
  • Profile information: school, state, weekly study goal
  • Usage data: study session duration, XP earned, league placement
  • Device information: FCM token for push notifications
  • Messages: chat messages sent within study rooms

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display leaderboards, leagues, and friend activity
  • To send push notifications about study reminders and friend requests
  • To improve the app experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the app (Firebase/Google) or as required by law.

The following information is visible to other Studra users:

  • Username
  • League and XP (on leaderboards)
  • School (if provided)
  • Study room activity

5. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for study reminders, friend requests, and league results. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Studra is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Deleting your account will permanently remove your profile, study history, and all associated data from our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of push notifications

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Studra after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
