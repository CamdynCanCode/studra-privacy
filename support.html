Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to the app → Profile → Settings → Account Actions → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

I didn't receive my verification email.

Check your spam folder. You can request a new one from the verification screen in the app. Make sure you're checking the correct email address.

How do leagues work?

Leagues reset every Sunday. The top performers in each league are promoted to the next tier, and the bottom performers are demoted. XP earned during the week determines your ranking.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Friends tab and search by username, or share your QR code from your profile for others to scan.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Studra → Cancel Subscription. You can also go to Settings inside the app and tap Manage Subscription.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

We'd love to hear from you. Email us at camdyncancode@gmail.com with details and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.
